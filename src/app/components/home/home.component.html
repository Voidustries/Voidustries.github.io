Speeds up development

Working with more people on one project gives different points of view that you may not have thought of. If you want to make something amazing, you have to get a lot of people involved. More people means more commits, more bug fixes, and a faster push to releases, for individual, non funded projects.

Community

Everyone that contributes to Voidustries has been reviewed and is willing to help. We do not restrict content but we do make sure those that wear our name will not tarnish the Voidustries name or personal projects name. Everyone that wears our name is always ready to help out when they can, and we try to keep trolls out as best we can.

Join Us

We are always looking for more people to interact with and join Voidustries. We welcome all and invite all to come and help out where ever they can.
